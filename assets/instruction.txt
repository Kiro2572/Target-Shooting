Controls:
  LMB         ==>   Shoot
  RMB         ==>   Reload
  Scroll Up   ==>   Next Weapon
  Scroll Down ==>   Previous Weapon
  Space       ==>   Pause/Unpause Game
  * Hold LMB to shoot continously on some weapon
  
Targets:
  Red Target     ==>   Get Scores
  Yellow Target  ==>   Get Bullets
  Green Target   ==>   Get Weapon
  * Target will disappear after 5 seconds
  * Losing a target will decreasing your score
  
Lose Conditions:
  Your score decreasing until it's below 0
  You let 20 target disappear
  You don't have any weapon left
